SUSTAINABLE DANISH DESIGN

We create Danish designer handbags for the colorful & bold modern day woman, while caring for the planet & its people.

OUR VALUES

VEGAN

In 2019, we presented our first vegan option in a collection, and we have continued to this since then.

RPET

In 2020 we are expecting to launch a new collection manufactured in rPET, recycled polyethylene terephthalate.

WORKING CONDITIONS

We believe it is our job to amplify a healthy and socially responsible conduct within our organization, including all our suppliers.

COTTON

Our cotton is either made from eco-friendly or organic cotton. Both materials require far less water consumption than conventional cotton products.

NEW! SILFEN PLAY

SILFEN Play is a subline to SILFEN which purpose is to create a new identity featuring a sporty universe with a common thread to SILFEN. SILFEN Play hits diversity - both the sporty and classic person. The two yearly collections represent sporty classics with a playful approach that have a longer lifespan. The collections will introduce both bags and clothes where sustainability is an integral part.

OUR STORY

SILFEN is a Danish accessory brand founded in 2013. The brand is characterized by high quality products created in genuine materials at reasonable prices.

We reflect the trends of each season and include timeless classics, offering something for everyone. We love playing with a wide range of textures and materials and continuously experiment to develop ourselves and our brand.
