NEW! SILFEN PLAY

SILFEN Play is a subline to SILFEN which purpose is to create a new identity featuring a sporty universe with a common thread to SILFEN.

SILFEN Play hits diversity - both the sporty and classic person. The two yearly collections represent sporty classics with a playful approach that have a longer lifespan. The collections will introduce both bags and clothes where sustainability is an integral part.

OUR STORY

SILFEN is a Danish accessory brand founded in 2013. The brand is characterized by high quality products created in genuine materials at reasonable prices.

We reflect the trends of each season and include timeless classics, offering something for everyone. We love playing with a wide range of textures and materials and continuously experiment to develop ourselves and our brand.
