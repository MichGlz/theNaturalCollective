Our universe

SILFEN is a Danish accessory brand founded in 2013. The brand is characterized by high quality products created in genuine materials at reasonable prices. We reflect the trends of each season and include timeless classics, offering something for everyone. We love playing with a wide range of textures and materials and continuously experiment to develop ourselves and our brand.
